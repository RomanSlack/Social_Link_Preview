Free Social Media Link Preview Tool

Social Link Preview is the fastest, free, no-signup open graph preview tool on the web. Instantly preview how your links, URLs, and web pages appear when shared on Google Search, Facebook, X (formerly Twitter), Discord, LinkedIn, Slack, iMessage, and WhatsApp. Built for developers, marketers, SEO specialists, social media managers, and content creators who need to test and debug their Open Graph (OG) tags, Twitter Card meta tags, and social sharing metadata before publishing.

Open Graph Tag Previewer & Debugger

Test your og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url, and og:site_name tags across every major platform in one click. No more switching between Facebook's Sharing Debugger, Twitter Card Validator, and LinkedIn Post Inspector. Social Link Preview combines all social media preview tools into a single, fast interface. Check your Open Graph protocol implementation, validate meta tags, and see exactly how your content will render when shared.

Twitter Card Validator & X Preview Tool

Preview both summary and summary_large_image Twitter Card types. See how your twitter:card, twitter:title, twitter:description, and twitter:image meta tags render on X (Twitter). Test Twitter Card markup without tweeting. Validate your Twitter meta tags instantly. Works as a replacement for the deprecated Twitter Card Validator tool.

Facebook Share Debugger Alternative

Preview your Facebook link shares without needing a Facebook account. See how og:title, og:description, and og:image display in Facebook's news feed. Debug Facebook sharing issues. Test Facebook Open Graph tags. Alternative to Facebook's Sharing Debugger and Open Graph Object Debugger that requires no login.

Discord Embed Preview

See exactly how your link appears as a Discord embed message. Preview Discord's rich embed cards with theme-color border, site name, title, description, and image. Test how Discord bot embeds and link previews render. Debug Discord unfurl issues and embed formatting.

LinkedIn Post Preview & Inspector

Preview how your shared links appear on LinkedIn feeds and posts. Test LinkedIn article sharing cards. Debug LinkedIn Post Inspector issues. See your og:image aspect ratio rendering on LinkedIn. Validate meta tags for LinkedIn sharing optimization.

Slack Link Unfurl Preview

Test how your URLs unfurl in Slack channels and direct messages. Preview Slack's link attachment formatting with site name, title, description, and thumbnail. Debug Slack bot link previews and unfurl cards.

iMessage & WhatsApp Link Preview

See how your links appear in iMessage conversations on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Preview WhatsApp link sharing cards with image, title, and description. Test rich link previews for mobile messaging apps. Debug Apple Messages link preview and WhatsApp URL card rendering.

SEO Meta Tag Checker & Validator

Validate all essential meta tags for SEO and social sharing: title tags, meta descriptions, canonical URLs, Open Graph tags, Twitter Cards, theme-color, favicons, and structured data. Essential tool for technical SEO audits, content marketing, website optimization, and social media marketing. Check meta tag character limits, preview SERP snippets, and optimize click-through rates from social media shares.
